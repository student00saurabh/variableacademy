Welcome to Physics Hub

Physics is the foundation of our understanding of the universe, from the smallest subatomic particles to the vast galaxies in space. It explores the fundamental principles governing matter, energy, motion, and forces, shaping modern science and technology.

Whether you're a student, a researcher, or a science enthusiast, this page provides comprehensive study materials, key concepts, important formulas, and real-world applications of physics. Dive into the fascinating world of physics and discover how it influences everything around us!

Explore various topics, download study resources, and enhance your knowledge of the subject that explains the mysteries of nature.

Fundamentals of Physics

Physics is the study of the natural world, dealing with matter, energy, motion, and forces. Understanding fundamental concepts like Newton's Laws, Thermodynamics, and Electromagnetism helps build a strong foundation in science and engineering.

Branches of Physics

Physics is divided into multiple branches, each focusing on different aspects of the physical world:
Classical Mechanics – Motion, forces, and Newtonian physics.
Thermodynamics – Heat, energy transfer, and entropy.
Electromagnetism – Electric and magnetic fields, circuits, and waves.
Quantum Mechanics – Behavior of particles at atomic and subatomic levels.
Relativity – Space, time, and gravitational effects (Einstein’s theory).
Optics – Light, reflection, refraction, and optical instruments.
